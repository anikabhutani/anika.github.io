Posted on 9/5/2020 4:00 pm:

Today, I started rereading the Maze Runner Series. It's so weird rereading something and knowing what'll happen soon. For example, in the Maze Runners, he talks about how he remembers the Glade and how that's something weird. He also remembers Teresa from somewhere. Even though most of the story is rusty in parts of my brain, I think Thomas was really involved in the planning behind the Glade and he was actually excited to go there and fix the disease thingy (I forgot the name of it hehe). As for Teresa *SPOILER* I know she dies in the 3rd book which is kinda sad but at the same time kinda relieving because doesn't she happen to be with the bad guys or something? I forgot but Thomas gets realls mad at her.



Posted on 8/7/2020 2:02 pm:

Sometimes, I really want to go to Hogwarts. It seems so nice to get a letter and go to Diagon Alley to get things like a wand, spellbooks, potions materials, robes, and more. I would also really want an owl. And you arrive to be sorted into houses of friendly people (except perheps slytherin). After eating a luxurious feast, you are whisked away to your dormitory with nice big beds and your stuff laid out for you. And along with that, you get your very own scarf and gear from your house to cheer them on during things like Quiddich and on a daily basis. Even the classes, despite the horrendous amounts of homework, seem enjoyable. So, yeah. Hogwarts seems super nice :)
